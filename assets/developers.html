This page contains cottage and house designs presented by participants of the "House to House" exhibition. Planning to build your own country house? We're sure to find the perfect option here. or you can find contacts of a reliable construction company that will develop an individual project taking into account all the wishes, or modify the standard project to meet the required criteria.

Geography of construction

Despite the fact that the exhibition and the head offices of most construction companies are located in the capital, You can order a house construction not only in Moscow and the surrounding region, but also in any region of the Russian Federation – from Kaliningrad to Sakhalin.

Many companies, in addition to their offices, have production facilities in the regions, which allows them to reduce costs of transporting building materials, as well as providing jobs for the required number of qualified workers construction crews.

Project filters

This page contains several thousand house and cottage designs. To avoid having to manually sort through them all, you can filter designs by your desired criteria – number of floors, area, architectural style, construction technology, and other features. On the left side of the screen, simply select where to position the filter sliders, and the site will display the houses and cottages you need.

When choosing the number of floors for your future home, it's best to discuss this with a construction company manager. They will explain the advantages and disadvantages of each option in more detail and, depending on your needs, suggest the optimal solution.

If, for example, you've always dreamed of building a house with a terrace, there's no need to waste time browsing and sorting through unnecessary options. Select the appropriate filter, and the website will display all houses designed with this extension.

How to find a construction company and order exchange

The main goal of a house exhibition is to bring together a reliable construction company and those who need its services. This means that the client will conclude the construction contract directly with the construction company whose representatives are present at the exhibition.

To simplify communication between the client and construction companies, each project page provides the latter's contact information. The project card indicates which construction technology can be used to build the selected house and which companies provide such services.

However, if necessary, the exhibition can help you choose a project. There's a hotline for this, which you can call yourself, or leave a request for a call back. When you contact an operator, simply tell them the nature of your order, and they will either suggest which company to contact or post your request on the house exhibition exchange, where all participants will see it and submit their price and timeline proposals.
