About the company "Terem"

Since 2010, the construction company "Terem" has been designing and constructing buildings from laminated veneer lumber, aerated concrete blocks and using frame technology. She has built over 50,000 cottages, summer houses, bathhouses, and small сbuildings in the Moscow region and other regions.

The company's catalog contains 720 projects. Each one can be modified: add a terrace or veranda, a room or an entire floor, move the walls a little or change the layout.

Today, Terem provides a full range of services: construction of wooden houses, individual design, Completion and reconstruction, installation of utilities, lending and insurance, land selection, and much more.
